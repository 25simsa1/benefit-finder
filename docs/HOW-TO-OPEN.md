# How to open Benefit Finder (Mac)

You do not need to install anything. Just follow these steps once.

## Step 1. Unzip it

Double-click the file **Benefit Finder-mac.zip** you were sent. This
creates an app called **Benefit Finder**. You can drag it to your
Applications folder or leave it wherever you like.

## Step 2. Open it the first time

The very first time only, Mac will not open it by a normal double-click,
because the app is not from the App Store. This is normal and safe.

1. **Right-click** (or hold Control and click) the **Benefit Finder** app.
2. Choose **Open** from the menu.
3. A box appears saying it cannot verify the developer. Click **Open**.

If you do not see an Open button, go to the Apple menu → **System
Settings** → **Privacy & Security**, scroll down, and click **Open
Anyway** next to Benefit Finder, then try again.

## Step 3. Use it

Your web browser (Safari or Chrome) will open by itself and show
Benefit Finder. Fill in the questions about your household and it will
show you the programs you may qualify for.

After the first time, you can just **double-click** the app normally and
it will open right up.

## To close it

When you are done, quit the app:

- Find **Benefit Finder** in the Dock at the bottom of your screen.
- Right-click its icon and choose **Quit** (or press Command-Q while it
  is the active app).

Closing the browser tab alone does not stop it, so use Quit.

## Is my information private?

Yes. Everything stays on your own computer. Benefit Finder does not use
the internet and does not send your information anywhere. Nothing is
saved unless you choose to export your profile yourself.

## If something looks wrong

This tool is a screener, not an official decision. Program rules and
income limits change every year. Every result shows a source link so you
can check the current rules before relying on it.
